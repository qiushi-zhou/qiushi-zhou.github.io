I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Computer Science, Aarhus University. I work with Dr. Ken Pfeuffer and Prof. Hans Gellersen on novel interaction techniques in Extended Reality (XR). Previously, I obtained my PhD at The University of Melbourne, advised by Eduardo Velloso and Jorge Goncalves. My research interest is creating deeper connections between human embodied perceptions and interactive technologies.
